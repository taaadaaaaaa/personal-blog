Hometown

Hanoi - fantastic, bustling, but peaceful city to live

Hanoi, Vietnam's capital, is the most popular and attractive city in the country's north. Furthermore, traditional Vietnamese culture can still be seen in many parts of Hanoi, giving you a more real experience while you're here. Besides, Hanoi has got a lot of sightseeing, such as The Long Bien Bridge, The Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum, Hoan Kiem Lake, etc. Last but not least, Hanoi cuisine is also sensational, such as Dogmeat, Pho, Bun Cha, etc. On the whole, you should visit Hanoi at least one time in your life, I guarantee that you won't regret when spending your precious time enjoying the exquisite beauty of Hanoi.
